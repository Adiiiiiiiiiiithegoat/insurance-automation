# Brand & Colour Scheme

These colours are taken (approximately) from the Tameen screenshots and are the
starting palette for the employee UI. They can be refined by colour-picking the
exact pixels from the logo once it is added.

## Palette
- Primary red (buttons, accents, highlights): `#E5174B`  ← the Tameen crimson red
- Dark navy (headings, primary text): `#1B2A4E`
- Near-black (top navigation bar): `#0B0B0C`
- Light lavender (page background): `#E9EDF7`
- White (cards / panels): `#FFFFFF`
- Muted grey (secondary text): `#6B7280`
- Success green (premium match banner): `#1D9E75`
- Warning red (premium mismatch banner): `#D64545`

## Logo
- Logo file location: `ui_assets/logo/` (e.g. `logo.png`)
- Show the logo top-left on every screen of the employee UI.

## Style direction
- Clean, modern, lots of white space.
- Rounded corners on cards and buttons.
- Red primary buttons with white text (matching the Tameen "Search" / "Payments by Channel" buttons).
- Navy headings, grey secondary text.
